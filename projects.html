My Elite Projects

A collection of my digital creations, where code meets creativity.

Sirvify

Sirvify is a B2B software solution close to my heart! This platform empowers companies to create dynamic campaigns using Email, SMS, WhatsApp, or a blend of the three. With customizable accounts and subscription plans, businesses can purchase credits tailored to their needs and send targeted messages to clients, either through bulk campaigns or direct communication.

I’m proud to have contributed to every stage of its development—from implementing messaging services and APIs to testing and deployment. Watching the idea blossom into a tool that companies use to boost their outreach has been incredibly rewarding.

Xact

Xact was my first professional project, and what an adventure it was! Built in collaboration with a fellow fresh grad under the insightful guidance of my manager, this React.js admin dashboard allowed our admins to sign up, authenticate, view client data, and perform CRUD operations effortlessly. One of my proudest contributions was implementing a live chat feature, enabling real-time communication with clients via WhatsApp!

Though the dashboard has since evolved into a financial products offering page for our company, the experience taught me valuable lessons in teamwork and real-world application development.

GoonMart

Need a shopping spree without leaving your chair? My talented friends and I built GoonMart for our final year Software Development course. This React.js online store offers users a seamless shopping experience where they can sign up, browse a diverse range of items, and easily search and filter products to find exactly what they need. With the ability to add items to their carts, shoppers can enjoy a taste of e-commerce magic!

While the checkout process is a mock-up, we focused on making the entire experience as realistic and engaging as possible. So, whether you’re on the hunt for the latest gadgets or just browsing for fun, GoonMart has something for everyone.
